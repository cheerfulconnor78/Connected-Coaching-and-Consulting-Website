Confident Kids Coaching Program

Whether or not kids believe in themselves will shape who they are and what they create in their lives. Powerful self-confidence isn't something kids either have or don't have. Believing in themselves, even after they've experienced a disappointment, is a skill that they can develop!

This program consists of 5 coaching sessions as follows to help kids learn how to believe in themselves when facing new things, move through fear, uncertainty and self-doubt, prepare their mind for success with "Power Shifting" and self-talk skills:

  • Session 1 - What is self-confidence
  • Session 2 - How Your Mind Shapes Your World
  • Session 3 - Neural Pathways & Managing Fear
  • Session 4 - Self-talk
  • Session 5 - Power Shifting

Personalized Coaching Program

It consists of personalized individual sessions tailored to the specific needs of your child. A consultation plus a questionnaire to be completed by both parent and child will reveal what life skills to focus on and where to begin.

You can book an one-off introductory session before committing to a full program to help you and your child feel comfortable to further discover growth mindset and life skills.
