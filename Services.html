Confident Kids Coaching Program

Whether or not kids believe in themselves will shape who they are and what they create in their lives. Powerful self-confidence isn't something kids either have or don't have. Believing in themselves, even after they've experienced a disappointment, is a skill that they can develop!

This program consists of 5 coaching sessions as follows to help kids learn how to believe in themselves when facing new things, move through fear, uncertainty and self-doubt, prepare their mind for success with "Power Shifting" and self-talk skills:

  • Session 1 - What is self-confidence
  • Session 2 - How Your Mind Shapes Your World
  • Session 3 - Neural Pathways & Managing Fear
  • Session 4 - Self-talk
  • Session 5 - Power Shifting

Personalized Coaching Program

It consists of personalized individual sessions tailored to the specific needs of your child. A consultation plus a questionnaire to be completed by both parent and child will reveal what life skills to focus on and where to begin.

You can book an one-off introductory session before committing to a full program to help you and your child feel comfortable to further discover growth mindset and life skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is life coaching for kids?

Life coaching for kids is about empowering young people to live their best lives. Just as a sports coach helps athletes develop skills and confidence, a life coach guides children in developing their mindset skills. They learn to harness the power of their thoughts to cultivate happiness, confidence, and success.

How is life coaching different from counseling?

While counseling addresses deeper issues like anxiety or trauma, life coaching focuses on helping children achieve their goals and aspirations. It's about creating the future they desire, rather than resolving past issues.

Isn't that the parent's job?

Parenting plays a vital role, but life coaching goes beyond traditional parenting. It focuses on teaching children personal development and leadership skills, empowering them to become self-directed individuals. Life coaches work in partnership with parents to support children's growth and success.

My child does't have a problem ... is life coaching for him?

Life coaching benefits all children. It equips them with valuable skills to navigate challenges and reach their full potential. These skills are invaluable throughout their lives.

What is the Teaching Method of the Course?

Currently, all courses we offer are delivered in the form of online Zoom meetings. However, if you live in Tokyo and would prefer your child to have in-person coaching sessions, we can arrange that. Please feel free to contact us for more information.
