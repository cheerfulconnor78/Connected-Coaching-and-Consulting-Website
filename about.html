About Le

Challenge means both risk and opportunity.

Whether it's asking questions in class, giving presentations or performances in front of teachers and classmates, applying for school teams, or signing up for various competitions, children face challenges almost every day that make them feel timid or nervous. Do they dare to step out of their comfort zone and pursue self-growth, or are they afraid of failure and avoid risks? How do you hope to help them make this choice?

However, sometimes it's not the child's choice, and they have to face life's challenges. Immigrating overseas with their parents, parental divorce, the death of a loved one, moving and transferring schools, etc. How do you hope to help them go through these challenges when they are faced with unintentional changes in their environment?

I'm Le, a life coach who helps children who are hesitant to step forward in the face of challenges or feel lost when they enter into challenges to bravely move forward. I worked for several Fortune 500 companies for over a decade and served as a decisive leader. Out of my passion for self-growth, I switched my career path and became a life coach. In the process of coaching my clients to improve themselves and solve life's problems, I found that many of the limiting beliefs that hinder their growth were formed in childhood, and our education lacked specific and practical training in children's emotional intelligence and mindset development.

I hope to help children improve their self-awareness and management of emotions, develop their mindset skills, enhance their confidence, in order to embrace challenges, ignite unlimited potential, and live a positive and vibrant life from a young age.

I am a certified coach by the International Coach Federation (ICF) and a certified WISDOM Coach with The Adventures in Wisdom Life Coaching Program for Kids Curriculum™. I hold a master's degree in social psychology and a master's degree in business administration. Born in Shanghai, I experienced my studies and career development in Japan and Hong Kong, and currently reside in Tokyo.
